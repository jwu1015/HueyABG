# Huy's tips: how to rizz up ABGs

## Do's

- Be genuine. Compliments work when they're specific and real (e.g. "I love how you styled that" not "you're hot").
- Listen more than you talk. Ask follow-up questions about her interests, work, or what she's into.
- Confidence > looks. Stand straight, make eye contact, speak clearly. Don't over-apologize or put yourself down.
- Humor is key. Light teasing, wit, and not taking yourself too seriously go a long way.
- Match her energy. If she's chill, be chill. If she's hype, you can be a bit more hype too.
- Get the number or IG when the vibe is good. "Let's keep in touch" or "I'll hit you up" is enough.

## Don'ts

- Don't use pick-up lines that sound like you copied them from the internet.
- Don't talk over her or one-up her stories. No "actually" or "well, I..."
- Don't be needy. One text is enough; double-texting too soon looks desperate.
- Don't make it all about appearance. Notice her style, interests, or vibe, not just how she looks.
- Don't lie to impress. It always catches up and kills trust.

## Conversation starters that work

- "What are you up to tonight?" (simple, opens the door)
- "I noticed you're into [X]—how'd you get into that?" (shows you paid attention)
- "Who's your go-to artist right now?" (music is an easy topic)
- "What's the best spot you've been to lately?" (food/places = safe and fun)

## Red flags to avoid

- Being pushy about meeting up or going to her place.
- Making everything sexual or flirty too fast.
- Ignoring when she's busy or not that engaged.
- Talking only about yourself or your job/money.
